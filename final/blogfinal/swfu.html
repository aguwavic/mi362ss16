Star Wars: The Force Awakens Review

Star Wars: The Force Awakens was a nostalgia filled movie. From the second you see the scrolling text to the moment you finally see Luke Skywalker memories of the past begin to resurface. This movie was fun and entertaining throughout. While I feel that the tried heavily to make this movie nostalgic, I wish they would have added more originality to the movie. There is not much to say about this other that if you liked the past Star Wars movies you will enjoy this and if this is your first Star Wars movie you will also have a good time.
