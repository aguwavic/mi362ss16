DeadPool Review

Let me start of by saying Deadpool is an unexpectedly, amazing and hilarious movie. From the minute the movie begins you know that you are going to enjoy it. Constant one-liners and some humorous action scenes makes this movie fun to watch. This movie stays true to the fourth wall breaking Deadpool in the comics. The action of the movie was also top notch and brutal. The only bad thing I can say about the movie is that the villains were weak and seemingly stood no chance against Deadpool. Overall I would recommend this movie to anyone.
